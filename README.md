## Dataset
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/aryanmdev/world-gym-and-fitness-trends-20002026

## Features
- `year`: the calendar year of the observation, from 2000 to 2026
- `gym_memberships`: total number of people with an active gym membership in that country and year
- `fitness_participation_rate`: share of the population that participates in any form of fitness activity
- `total_health_club_revenue_usd`: combined revenue generated by all gyms and health clubs in USD
- `number_of_gyms`: total count of gym and health club facilities in the country
- `gym_penetration_rate`: *target* — share of the total population that holds a gym membership
- `urban_population_percentage`: proportion of the population living in urban areas
- `obesity_rate`: share of the population classified as obese
- `gdp_per_capita_usd`: average economic output per person in USD, a proxy for wealth
- `population_total`: total population of the country
- `average_membership_cost_usd`: average monthly or annual cost of a gym membership
- `insufficient_physical_activity_pct`: share of the population that does not meet recommended physical activity levels
- `region`: geographic region the country belongs to (Africa, Asia, Europe, North America, Oceania, South America)
- `country`: name of the country
